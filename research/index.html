Research Interests

  • Generally speaking, my current research interests are studying high-dimensional math problems, mainly at the interface of probability, combinatorics, and information theory. Specifically, my research interests include:
    • Entropic/functional/isoperimetric inequalities
    • Packing and covering
    • Information-theoretic methods

Useful Techniques in High-Dimensional Combinatorics, Probability, and Information Theory
