Research Interests

  • Generally speaking, my current research interests are studying high-dimensional math problems, mainly at the interface of probability, combinatorics, and information theory. Specifically, my research interests include:
    • Combinatorics and Coding Theory
      • Boolean functions and Hamming spaces
      • Packing and covering
    • Probability and Information Theory
      • Common information, noise stability, isoperimetry, and hypercontractivity
      • Coupling and optimal transport
      • Information-theoretic methods, especially the combination of entropy/divergence and coupling techniques
  • Past Interests: My research has seamlessly transitioned from video transmission to current ones.
    (Thank my former advisors for providing me freedom to choose topics.)
